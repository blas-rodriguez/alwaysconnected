FAQ

Will this work on my phone?

You need an eSIM-compatible phone and an unlocked device. Most modern iPhones (XR/XS and newer) support eSIM. Many recent Android models do too.

Can I keep my WhatsApp number?

Yes. WhatsApp stays on your main number. You just use the eSIM for data.

Is eSIM better than buying a local SIM?

For many travelers, yes: it’s faster and more convenient. If you’ll stay long-term in one country, a local SIM can sometimes be cheaper. That’s why I recommend options by destination.

Do you provide the eSIM?

No. I recommend and compare trusted providers. Purchases happen on the provider’s website/app.
